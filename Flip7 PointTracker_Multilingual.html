Flip7 Rules

Goal of the game

Flip7 is a fast, tactical card game. The goal is to collect as many points as possible over several rounds without busting.

Round flow

  • Players take turns drawing cards.
  • There are Number cards (1–12) and Special cards (x2, +2, +4, +6, +8, +10, Flip 3, Flip 4, etc.).
  • You may keep drawing until you have exactly 7 different number cards (Flip 7) or you bust.

Scoring (official order)

  1. Sum the number cards (only unique numbers count)
  2. Apply x2 multiplier (if present) only to the number sum
  3. Add Bonus cards (+2 to +10)
  4. Flip 7 Bonus: +15 points if you have exactly 7 different number cards

Important rules

  • Each number (1–12) may appear only once per round.
  • If you draw a number you already have → Bust (0 points this round).
  • With Flip 7 (exactly 7 different numbers) your round ends automatically with +15 bonus.
  • The round ends for everyone as soon as someone reaches Flip 7.
